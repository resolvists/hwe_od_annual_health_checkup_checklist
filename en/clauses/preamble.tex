\section*{\centering ANNUAL HEALTH CHECKUP CHECKLIST}
\vspace*{0.5cm}

An annual health checkup structured around key body systems provides a comprehensive framework for preventive care and early risk detection. By evaluating cardiovascular, respiratory, metabolic, neurological, musculoskeletal, immune, and reproductive functions in an organized manner, this checklist supports a holistic understanding of overall health status. System-based screening promotes early identification of emerging conditions, reinforces preventive strategies, and facilitates informed medical decision-making. Such a structured approach enhances continuity of care, strengthens long-term health planning, and contributes to improved overall well-being.
